Exercise I.2

What is the probability of drawing two aces in a row from a deck of fifty-two cards?

Solution

Let \(a\) be the number of aces in a deck of cards, let \(d\) be the number of cards in a deck. The author demonstrates the probability of drawing one ace from a deck of fifty-two cards as $$ a \div d = 4 \div 52 = 0.077 = 7.7\% $$ The author also explains the probability of an event occuring multiple times in sequence. This is done by multiplying the individual probabilities of the events. So let \(p\) be the probability of drawing an ace from the deck of fifty-two cards. The probability of drawing an ace twice in a row is $$ p \cdot p = 0.077 \cdot 0.077 = 0.0059 = 0.006\% $$
